About Me

As a designer, my passion lies in creating designs that align with brand identities while emphasizing longevity. During my experience working with smaller brands, I not only crafted marketing campaigns but also implemented tutorials and templates to ensure a seamless design process for the future of the company. In my personal work, I thrive on pushing the boundaries and exploring alternative artistic styles that reflect the cutting edge of design culture. Beyond the realm of design, I embrace the beauty of Alberta's landscape as an avid rock climber. In my free time, you can find me hiking, camping, golfing, skiing or just enjoying a good patio beer.
